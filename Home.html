HAMRO SAHAR

Nestled in the heart of the Himalayas, Kathmandu, the capital city of Nepal, is a captivating blend of ancient traditions and modern aspirations. This vibrant metropolis serves as the cultural, historical, and economic hub of the nation, offering a rich tapestry of experiences for locals and travelers alike.One of Kathmandu's most distinctive features is its breathtaking setting. Surrounded by majestic mountains, including the world-famous peaks of the Himalayan range, the city's landscape is both awe-inspiring and humbling. The snow-capped peaks stand as sentinels, watching over the valley and providing a constant reminder of Nepal's geographical grandeur.
